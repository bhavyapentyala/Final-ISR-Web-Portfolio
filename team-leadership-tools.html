👥 Team Leadership & HR Tools

3-Day Onboarding Plan

Day 1 – Orientation & Setup

  • Team introduction & IT Supervisor meeting
  • Company mission, policies, and HR orientation
  • System setup: Email, VPN, Jira, chat, and ticketing
  • Overview of help tools and knowledge base

Day 2 – Shadowing & Familiarization

  • Shadow Tier 1 technician for ticket triage
  • Explore Jira and escalation levels
  • Join stand-up meetings and understand KPIs (FCR, CSAT)

Day 3 – Hands-On & Expectations

  • Resolve 2–3 real tickets with supervision
  • Document ticket handling process
  • Supervisor feedback & 30-day KPI goal planning

PTO Request & Approval Tracker

The Paid Time Off (PTO) Request and Approval Tracker is an essential HR tool used to monitor employee availability. By logging request types, date ranges, approval statuses, and supervisor notes, the tracker ensures effective shift planning and compliance with internal HR policies. It helps teams avoid resource shortages during critical periods, improves workload distribution, and enhances workforce transparency. Whether dealing with medical emergencies or vacation plans, this tracker supports proactive team leadership and well-being management across departments.
